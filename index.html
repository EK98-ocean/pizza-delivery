About Pizza Kenya

Pizza Kenya started as a small, family-owned pizzeria in Nairobi, Kenya, in 1995. Since then, we have grown to over 10 branches. We believe our pizzas are the best in the region, which is why we have introduced this website, to make sure everyone has the opportunity to try our pizzas, wherever they are. Simply scroll down and place your order with us, and we'll deliver it right to your doorstep.
