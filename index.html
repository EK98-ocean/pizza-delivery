About Pizza Kenya

Pizza Kenya started as a small, family-owned pizzeria in Nairobi, Kneya, in 1995. Since then, we have grown to over 10 branches. We believe our pizzas are the best in Kenya, which is why we have introduced this website, to make sure everyone has the oppoertunity to try our pizzas, wherever you are. Simply scroll down and place your order with us, and we'll deliver it wehrever you are.
